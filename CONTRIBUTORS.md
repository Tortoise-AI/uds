## Contribution Agreement

All contributions to UDS are made under the terms of the [Apache License 2.0](./LICENSE). By submitting a contribution, you agree that your work will be licensed under the same terms.

### Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

A formal Contributor License Agreement (CLA) will be required for all contributions. The CLA ensures that:

- Contributors retain ownership of their contributions
- Tortoise AI Ltd and the community can use, modify, and distribute contributions
- All contributions are properly licensed and free of legal encumbrances
- The project maintains clear intellectual property rights

The CLA process will be established in a future phase. Until then, all contributions are accepted under the standard Apache 2.0 license terms.

## How to Be Listed

Contributors will be added to this list when their pull requests are merged. Contributions include:

- Specification improvements and clarifications
- Documentation enhancements
- Example dashboards
- Tooling (parsers, renderers, validators)
- Bug reports and issue discussions
- Community support and advocacy

## Questions

For questions about contributing or the CLA process, please open an issue in the repository or contact the maintainers.
